\chapter{Reinforcement Learning}
\label{chap:reinforcement_learning}

This chapter introduces the fundamental concepts of reinforcement learning, providing the necessary foundation for the ideas developed throughout the rest of this manuscript.  
This chapter can be seen as a synthesis of the standard references in reinforcement learning, in particular \textit{Reinforcement Learning: An Introduction} \cite{sutton2018reinforcement}.
Its structure is as follows:

\begin{itemize}
    \item \textbf{Historical developments:} an overview of the key ideas and milestones that led to the emergence of reinforcement learning as a unified field.
    \item \textbf{Ojective:} the mathematical definition of the reinforcement learning goal through Markov Decision Processes, policies, and value functions.
    \item \textbf{Learning:} how policies are progressively refined through interaction with the environment to approach the optimal policy.
\end{itemize}
