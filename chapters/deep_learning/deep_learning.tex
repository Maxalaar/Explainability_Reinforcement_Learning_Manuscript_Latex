\chapter{Deep Learning}

\glsresetall

This chapter introduces the fundamental concepts of \gls{dl}, providing the necessary foundation for the ideas developed throughout the rest of this manuscript.
This chapter can be seen as a synthesis of the reference work \emph{Deep Learning} \cite{goodfellow2016deep}.
Its structure is as follows:

\begin{itemize}
    \item A overview of the historical developments that led to the emergence of the field.
    \item A formalization of the objective underlying \gls{dl} algorithms.
    \item An examination of the structure of artificial \gls{nn}.
    \item An explanation of how the learning process shapes \gls{nn}.
    \item A discussion of how such models are evaluated.
\end{itemize}
