\chapter{Deep Learning}
\label{chap:deep_learning}

\glsresetall

This chapter introduces the fundamental concepts of \gls{dl}, providing the necessary foundation for the ideas developed throughout the rest of this manuscript.
This chapter can be seen as a synthesis of the reference work \underline{Deep Learning} \cite{goodfellow2016deep}.
Its structure is as follows:

\begin{itemize}
    \item[]Section~\ref{sec:historical_developments}: An overview of the historical developments that led to the emergence of the field.
    \item[]Section~\ref{sec:objective}: A formalization of the objective underlying \gls{dl} algorithms.
    \item[]Section~\ref{sec:neural_networks_structure}: An examination of the structure of artificial \gls{nn}.
    \item[]Section~\ref{sec:learning}: An explanation of how the learning process shapes \gls{nn}.
    \item[]Section~\ref{sec:evaluation}: A discussion of how such models are evaluated.
\end{itemize}
