        Illustration of entanglement in the latent space of a generative face
        model \cite{haas2024discovering}.
        The top rows show entangled directions: moving along the \emph{glasses}
        direction simultaneously modifies age and gender, and moving along the
        \emph{age} direction also alters gender. This reflects the entanglement
        phenomenon, where a single direction in latent space induces unintended
        changes across multiple semantic concepts at once.
        The bottom rows show the corresponding disentangled directions, where
        each axis controls a single semantic attribute while leaving the others
        unchanged.
